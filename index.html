LETS GO

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Click on the fretboard layout to input a triad/arpeggio/scale/chord (the computer will treat the first note you input as the root note)
  2. Press 'input new chord' button to input the next triad/arpeggio/scale/chord
  3. Press the 'start animation' button to visualize the whole fretboard
  4. Press 'Next chord/Previous chord' to visualize how the shapes transition and morph into new shapes
  5. Press 'show intervals' button to see the colour coded scale degrees across the board
  6. Press 'start again' to input a new chord progression

I'm Adesh, Im a guitar player and a computer programming enthusiast based in Bangalore.I currently teach guitar at TAAQADEMY music school.

The FretNoodle visualizer was designed and programmed by me in order to help me visualize chord changes in new ways,generate new ideas for tasteful improvisation and also help me better understand the solos that I transcribe and their relationship of each note with the harmony. On realizing how useful a tool this is for guitar teachers and advancing players, I decided to make it publicly available through this website. FretNoodle visualizer is subject to constant improvement and changes, I am open to feedback and suggestions to help make the best fretboard visualizer for you.
